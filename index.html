Hotel Booking Terms & Conditions

  • You may book your accommodation between 21 Sept 2024 and 27 Sept 2024. If you need any assistance in pre or postdates booking, please write to accommodation@mpsnewdelhi.org.
  • The room rates are on a per room per night basis and non-cumulative.
  • The above-mentioned rates are only valid if booked through the conference secretariat and MPS 2024 website.
  • The rates are inclusive of all GST as applicable (currently 18%) and buffet breakfast at the designated restaurant.
  • Confirm room booking is possible against 100% advance only.
  • The rates are exclusive of Airport Transfers, Tips, Laundry, Meals not mentioned, Room Service, Mini Bar, Phone Bills, or any other services not mentioned above.
  • Room check-in at 14:00 hrs and check-out at 12:00 hrs. In case of early arrival, room(s) to be booked from the previous day for immediate occupancy.
  • In case of late check-out, full night charges will be applicable and will be subject to availability.
  • Maximum 01 or 02 Guests per room are allowed as per occupancy reserved.
  • Additional Bed or Mattress will be chargeable.
  • Rooms will be allotted on a first-come, first-serve basis and are subject to availability at the time of booking.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation received before 31 March – No retention
  • Cancellation received between 1 April 2024 to 15 June 2023 – 50% of accommodation charges charged as retention
  • Cancellation received from 16 July 2023 onwards till conference dates – 100% of total accommodation charges charged as retention
